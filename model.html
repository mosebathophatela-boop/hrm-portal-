Our Approach

HOW WE MAKE MONEY

A SaaS subscription model built on product-led growth — users discover the value themselves, then upgrade.

🆓

Freemium Entry

The free Starter plan is deliberately generous — enough for individuals and small teams to experience real value. No credit card, no friction. Just a great product doing its job.

📈

Viral Expansion

Every summary sent, every workflow triggered, every report shared — exposes non-users to Spark Labs. Our product spreads itself organically across organisations.

💳

Subscription Revenue

Teams that grow beyond free tier limits upgrade to Pro at $49/user/month. Our best-in-class conversion rate is 18% — 3× the SaaS industry average of 6%.

🏢

Enterprise Upsell

Once we've proven value at the team level, Enterprise sales motion lands company-wide contracts — often 5-10× the initial ARR — with custom pricing and dedicated support.
